depuis 2018

A part of France in Putney

In 2018, our French bakers set up their ovens at 64 Lower Richmond Road with a simple idea: bake everything on the premises, the way it is done back home. Real butter, patient dough, no shortcuts.

Word travelled fast. Saturday-morning croissant runs became a Putney ritual, the croque monsieur earned a reputation across south-west London, and the little shop with the green front became a place where neighbours actually know each other.

Today the counter still fills before sunrise, the vintage china still hangs above the coffee machine, and the galette des rois still arrives every January with its golden crowns.

Draft narrative, to be confirmed and enriched with the client's real story, names and dates.
